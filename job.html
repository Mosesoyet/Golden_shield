GOLDEN SHIELD SECURITY SERVICE LTD TRAINING

Our success is based on an extensive selection process. Our selection, Vetting and training procedures are exhaustive but not limited to,

  • - Police finger print and presentation of National ID by the applicant to confirm he/she is a South Sudanese
  • - Reference letter from the Chief
  • - Minimum qualification of S3 or S4 and age between 22 - 50 years old.

Guards have to meet our employment criteria and pass our training course before employment. Our training has been developed covering all aspects of security.

Our security training packages at different levels are updated continously as methods and threats keep changing. Innitial training is never enough and therefore all personnel will undergo refresher training both residental and onsite.

We have specialist training courses for Basic guard training course, Supervisors, Vehicle Crew and for Refresher Training.

All Supervisors and Senior Guards will in addition to the basic course undergo training on:

  • ⇥ Leadership
  • ⇥ Report writing
  • ⇥ Investigation

Basic Security Guards Training Courses:

Canine Training

Dogs are selected on size, health, intelligence, personal characteristics and breed,
keeping the purpose of the dog in mind, we mainly specialize in German Shephard dogs for security because of their intelligence. Dog handlers are selected from the quard force and taken through a rigorous train.
